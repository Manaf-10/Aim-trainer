# 🎯 Aim Trainer
Aim Trainer is a simple 3D game designed to help you improve your shooting accuracy and reaction time.

🕹️ How to Play
Shoot: Click your Left Mouse Button to fire.

Reload: Press the 'R' key when you run out of ammo.

Score Points: * Hit the Center (Bullseye) for 25 points.

Hit the Body for 10 points.

Ammo: You have 12 bullets per magazine. Watch your ammo counter!
